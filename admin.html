Frequently Asked Questions

How is this actually free? What's the catch?

There's no catch. NEOquad was founded by two students who experienced the isolation problem firsthand. We've personally invested in building this because we believe every student deserves better tools to connect. We're committed to keeping our founding partner school free foreverâ€”that's a promise.

What data do you collect from students?

Only the essentials: name, school email, and an encrypted password. We don't track location beyond event check-ins, don't sell data, and don't run ads. Students can delete their account and all associated data at any time.

Who can create events on our campus?

Only administrators you designate. You provide us a list of approved club leader emails, and only those accounts can create events. You maintain complete controlâ€”we can add or remove posting privileges at your request anytime.

What if inappropriate content gets posted?

Since only your verified club leaders can post, this is rare. However, you'll have admin access to immediately remove any event. We also have reporting tools and can assist with moderation if needed.

Does IT need to be involved?

No IT integration required. neoQuad runs entirely independently. Students download from the App Store, sign in with their .edu emailâ€”that's it. No single sign-on configuration, no server setup, no maintenance.

How long does setup take?

Most campuses are live within 24 hours of providing their approved admin email list. We handle all technical configuration. Your only task is deciding who gets posting access.
